IIMA prof. bags National Film Award for Best Documentary as producer

The film God, Vulture and Human is a tribute to the role of organ transplant coordinators (OTCs) in the emotionally challenging process of organ donation and transplantation in India. The documentary explores the complex emotional landscape that organ transplant coordinators navigate daily as they work with families during some of their most difficult moments.

Professor at Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIMA) has achieved recognition at the national level for producing this impactful documentary that sheds light on an often overlooked but crucial aspect of healthcare in India. The film provides insight into the dedication and emotional resilience required by professionals who coordinate organ transplants, facilitating the gift of life between donors and recipients.

This recognition highlights the important intersection between academia, healthcare, and social awareness through documentary filmmaking.
